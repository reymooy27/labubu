About

Labubu is a captivating character born from the creative mind of Hong Kong artist Kasing Lung, introduced to the world in 2015. With its distinctive pointed ears, sharp teeth, and mischievous grin, Labubu may look like a troublemaker, but beneath that exterior lies a playful, kind-hearted personality. Labubu hails from "The Monsters" collection, a universe inspired by fairy tales and Nordic mythology. This doll embodies a whimsical blend of light and dark, representing the quirky charm of embracing life's imperfections.
